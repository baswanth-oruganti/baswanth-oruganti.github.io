Singular Set Σ
The Clifford Torus T² ⊂ S³ — Topological Barrier in TOPCT The Clifford torus is the equatorial singular set separating the two connected components of the frontier orbital Bloch sphere. It represents the HOMO-LUMO degeneracy locus — the Coulson-Fischer point — at which the coveted generator becomes accessible and symmetry breaking occurs. Woodward-Hoffmann forbidden reactions correspond to paths that must cross this topological barrier.

P6V30 Research Programme

Constraint-Induced Topological Barriers in Electronic Structure and Optimization

A six-pillar research programme (2025–2030) developing a unified geometric framework in which physically motivated constraints produce fibre bundle structures, and topological barriers govern transitions between symmetry-inequivalent states in quantum chemistry, optimization, and machine learning.
