About Me

Hello! My name is Baswanth Oruganti. I currently work as an Associate Professor in the School of Mathematics and Natural Sciences at Chanakya University, and I am based in Bangalore. My expertise spans Quantum Chemistry, Computational photohemistry, and Quantum Computing. I am passionate about teaching and research, with a particular focus on molecular design, quantum simulations, and aromaticity analysis.
